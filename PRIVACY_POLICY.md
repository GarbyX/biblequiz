# 📜 Bible Quiz – Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** October 2025

Thank you for using **Bible Quiz**.

## 🔒 Data Security
Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no external storage or processing risks.  
Your data stays securely on your phone.
